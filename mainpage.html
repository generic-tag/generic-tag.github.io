My favorite vacation destinations

Jeju Island (Jeju-do)

Photo by me

Jeju-do is an island off the southern coast of South Korea. I've been there multiple times

with my family,and I enjoygoing there every time. It's primarily known for its natural

lanscapes such as its beaches, mountains, lakes, etc. It also has a very sweet oramge called

"Hallabong". The latitude of Jeju-do is around the same as Los Angeles, but it can get a lot

more humid. I would recommend going there if you're ever in Korea.



Big Sur

Photo also by me

Big Sur, and its surrounding areas, was another one of my favorite places to visit while on

vacation. I enjoy its scenery, both on the way there and while there. Even though the direct

route is still closed due to the landslide on Pacific Coast Highway, the alternate doesn't

take too much time. One thing that I like to do while there is taking photographs of the

sea and the land surrounding it. However, cell reception will be spotty at best in some

areas, and no signal in other areas.
