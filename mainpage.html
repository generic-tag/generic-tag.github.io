Jeju Island (Jeju-do)

Photo by me

Jeju-do is an island off the southern coast of South Korea. I've been there multiple times

with my family,and I enjoygoing there every time. It's primarily known for its natural

lanscapes such as its beaches, mountains, lakes, etc. It also has a very sweet oramge called

"Hallabong". The latitude of Jeju-do is around the same as Los Angeles, but it can get a lot

more humid. I would recommend going there if you're ever in Korea.
